Introducing SeekCut 2

After years of neglect by its creator, SeekCut is back — rebuilt from the ground up with a brand-new edit-detection engine and a streamlined interface. It’s more accurate, smarter, and a whole lot easier on the eyes.

Designed for sound editors and re-recording mixers, SeekCut automatically detects video edits and exports precise MIDI marker files for import directly into Pro Tools. No guesswork, no banging on the + and – keys trying to find every cut. Just load a video, set your session start and video start times, hit Start Scan, and let SeekCut do the rest.

Best part? It’s free (although please do consider a small donation).

Tech Specs

  • macOS 14.6 (Sonoma) or later (sorry, no Windows)
  • Supports H.264 and ProRes videos
  • Accepted formats: .mov or .mp4
  • Not supported: Avid DNx (look for “AVdn” in Finder->Get Info under codecs) or .mxf files
